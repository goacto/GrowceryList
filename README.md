# GrowceryList

**Fuel Your Growth Journey Through Mindful Nutrition**

GrowceryList is a web application that connects your food choices to your personal growth journey. Part of the goacto ecosystem, it helps individuals and families track grocery lists while reflecting on how their nutrition choices support their goals.

## Core Concept

The food we consume is the literal fuel for our growth journey. GrowceryList helps you ask: "Will the fuel I'm accepting get me to where I want to go?"

## Features

- **Grocery List Management**: Create and manage weekly grocery lists with items, quantities, and costs
- **Growth Reflections**: Document why you chose specific items and how they support your growth goals
- **Recipe Integration**: Attach recipes and meal plans to your grocery lists
- **Community Sharing**: Share your lists, get inspired by others, and contribute to a growth-minded community
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Start with local storage, optionally create an account to sync and share

## Technology Stack

- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript (no framework dependencies)
- Progressive web application approach
- Educational codebase designed to demonstrate concepts from beginner to advanced
